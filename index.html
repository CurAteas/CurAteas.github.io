Welcome to CurAteas

CurAteas is a tea-centered experience brand rooted in wellness, community, and creative collaboration. Based in New York’s Capital Region, we offer small-batch iced teas, loose leaf blends, and pop-up tea services for events and partnerships.

Featured Blends

Peach Jasmine Green Tea

A sunny blend of green tea, jasmine, and real freeze-dried peaches. Slightly sweetened and bright with floral undertones, it's perfect for a refreshing pick-me-up.

Lavender Earl Grey

Classic bergamot black tea meets calming lavender. A grounding and aromatic experience, perfect for moments of clarity or calm.

Rose White Tea

Delicate white tea petals meet romantic rose for a soft, heart-opening brew that’s both elegant and soothing to sip.

Community & Values

We source ethically and locally whenever possible, and prioritize partnerships with women-, LGBTQIA-, and BIPOC-owned farms and vendors. Every sip supports a vision of connected wellness and equitable creativity.

Tea Experiences & Collaborations

CurAteas offers more than just tea—we bring people together through curated experiences designed to educate, celebrate, and connect.

Tea Education Workshops: Want to dive deeper into the world of tea? We offer guided workshops and classes covering tea basics, brewing techniques, ingredient sourcing, and tea wellness. Perfect for schools, wellness centers, or community groups seeking an interactive and enriching activity.

Private Tea Parties: Host a beautifully curated tea gathering for birthdays, holidays, bridal showers, or just because.
