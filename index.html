About CurAteas

CurAteas is your source for locally crafted, wellness-inspired tea blends. Our mission is to bring curated tea experiences to the Capital Region through ethically sourced ingredients and vibrant community connections.

Our Teas

Lavender Earl Grey

Classic black tea with bergamot and soothing lavender. A relaxing, aromatic brew perfect for mindful sipping.

Rose White Tea

Delicate white tea with rose petals. Soft, fragrant, and perfect for a quiet moment of beauty.
