About CurAteas

CurAteas was brewed from a passion for tea and a love for community.

Founded by Aniel, a former Teavana manager, and professional tea connoisseur and Evelyn an educator, and tea enthusiast; CurAteas is more than a tea company—it’s a curated experience rooted in education, sustainability, and connection. Each blend is thoughtfully crafted using responsibly sourced ingredients, with a focus on quality and wellness.

We believe tea should nourish more than your body—it should uplift your spirit, support local communities, and invite moments of calm in a busy world.

From handcrafted iced teas to loose-leaf favorites, CurAteas is committed to delivering tea that tastes good and does good. Whether you're new to tea or a seasoned sipper, we’re here to help you find your perfect cup.

Community Impact

Rooted in Community, Steeped in Purpose

At CurAteas, we believe tea is more than a beverage—it’s a catalyst for connection, healing, and equity. As an LGBTQIA-owned business, CurAteas is committed to cultivating a more inclusive, supportive, and vibrant community.

We prioritize working with women-, minority-, and LGBTQIA-owned partners and suppliers, investing directly in the people and values we believe in. From sourcing ingredients to selecting collaborators, every step is intentional—supporting local economies, ethical practices, and shared success.

Our mission includes not only providing quality tea but also sharing knowledge. Through tea education, wellness events, and future community programming, we aim to make the world of tea more accessible, healing, and inspiring.

When you sip CurAteas, you're supporting:

Inclusive business ownership and representation

Local sourcing and ethical partnerships

Education and tea literacy

Community wellness and engagement

Together, we can make every cup a chance to give back and grow forward.

Our Teas

Peach Jasmine Green Tea:

Bright and refreshing, this blend pairs delicate green tea leaves with fragrant jasmine blossoms and juicy notes of real peach. Lightly sweetened and perfectly balanced, it’s a calming sip that brings the essence of spring to every season. Ideal for iced tea lovers who enjoy floral complexity with a fruity finish.

Lavender Earl Grey:

A soothing twist on a timeless classic. Bold black tea meets aromatic bergamot and is softened with a touch of lavender for a floral, comforting cup. This blend is refined yet relaxing—perfect for slow mornings, thoughtful afternoons, or whenever you need a little calm in your cup.

Rose White Tea:

Elegant and subtle, this blend combines delicate white tea with the soft, romantic flavor of real rose petals. Naturally sweet and minimally processed, it’s a light-bodied tea that offers a gentle lift with a floral aroma. A beautiful choice for mindful sipping and peaceful moments.

Our Mission

At CurAteas, our mission is to create meaningful tea experiences that nourish both people and communities. We craft small-batch teas using responsibly sourced ingredients, blending quality, wellness, and sustainability in every cup.

Rooted in education and inspired by connection, CurAteas exists to bring people together—whether through thoughtful flavors, local partnerships, or giving back. We believe that tea isn’t just a drink—it’s a way to pause, share, and uplift.

We're building a movement founded in community, sustainability, and joy. Whether you're steeping your first leaves or are a seasoned tea connoisseur, we're here to support your journey.
