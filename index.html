Welcome to CurAteas

CurAteas was brewed from a passion for tea and a love for community.

Founded by Aniel, a former Teavana manager, local Tea Shop Trainer and professional tea connoisseur, and Evelyn an educator and tea enthusiast; CurAteas is more than a tea company—it’s a curated experience rooted in education, sustainability, and connection. Each blend is thoughtfully crafted using responsibly sourced ingredients, with a focus on quality and wellness.

We believe tea should nourish more than your body—it should uplift your spirit, support local communities, and invite moments of calm in a busy world.

From handcrafted iced teas to loose-leaf favorites, CurAteas is committed to delivering tea that tastes good and does good. Whether you're new to tea or a seasoned sipper, we’re here to help you find your perfect cup.

CurAteas is a tea-centered experience brand rooted in wellness, community, and creative collaboration. Based in New York’s Capital Region, we offer small-batch iced teas, loose leaf blends, and pop-up tea services for events and partnerships.

Tea Experiences & Collaborations

CurAteas offers more than just tea—we bring people together through curated experiences designed to educate, celebrate, and connect.

Tea Education Workshops: Want to dive deeper into the world of tea? We offer guided workshops and classes covering tea basics, brewing techniques, ingredient sourcing, and tea wellness. Perfect for schools, wellness centers, or community groups seeking an interactive and enriching activity.

Private Tea Parties: Host a beautifully curated tea gathering for birthdays, holidays, bridal showers, or just because. We offer intimate tea party setups for 2 to 20 guests, complete with custom blends, elegant presentation, and an optional educational component

Herbal Wisdom & Tea Education

At CurAteas, every blend is a conversation between ancient herbal knowledge and modern wellness. We believe in making tea education accessible and inspiring—because knowing what’s in your cup is just as powerful as drinking it.

Our Ingredients, Our Intentions:

  • Lavender: Calming and floral, it’s known to support restful sleep and reduce stress.
  • Rose: Rich in antioxidants, rose encourages emotional harmony and heart health.
  • Jasmine: Uplifting and aromatic, jasmine enhances mood and supports digestion.
  • White Tea: Light, minimally processed, and high in antioxidants for gentle focus.
  • Green Tea: Clean, grassy, and rich in L-theanine for calm energy and clarity.
  • Black Tea: Bold and grounding, great for heart health and mental stamina.
