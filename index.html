Welcome to CurAteas

We believe tea is more than a beverage — it is a bridge to wellness, a ritual of presence, and a practice of community care. Our teas are intentionally curated and brewed with ingredients that nourish, calm, energize, and uplift.

Founded by Nathaniel "Aniel" Galloway, CurAteas is an Albany-based mobile tea company delivering small-batch bottled teas and loose-leaf blends directly to our community. With every sip, we celebrate healing, heritage, and heart-forward entrepreneurship.

Our Signature Blends

    Our Teas

    Peach Jasmine Green Tea:

    Bright and refreshing, this blend pairs delicate green tea leaves with fragrant jasmine blossoms and juicy notes of real peach. Lightly sweetened and perfectly balanced, it’s a calming sip that brings the essence of spring to every season. Ideal for iced tea lovers who enjoy floral complexity with a fruity finish.

    Lavender Earl Grey:

    A soothing twist on a timeless classic. Bold black tea meets aromatic bergamot and is softened with a touch of lavender for a floral, comforting cup. This blend is refined yet relaxing—perfect for slow mornings, thoughtful afternoons, or whenever you need a little calm in your cup.

    Rose White Tea:

    Elegant and subtle, this blend combines delicate white tea with the soft, romantic flavor of real rose petals. Naturally sweet and minimally processed, it’s a light-bodied tea that offers a gentle lift with a floral aroma. A beautiful choice for mindful sipping and peaceful moments.

Our Mission

We are committed to sourcing from local and women-owned suppliers whenever possible. Our small batches are crafted with attention to quality, wellness benefits, and vibrant flavor. We aim to offer more than just tea — we offer space for healing, gathering, and empowerment through intentional sipping and storytelling.

Tea Experiences & Collaborations

CurAteas offers more than just tea—we bring people together through curated experiences designed to educate, celebrate, and connect.

Tea Education Workshops: Guided workshops and classes covering tea basics, brewing techniques, ingredient sourcing, and tea wellness—ideal for schools, wellness centers, or community groups.

Private Tea Parties: Elegant, custom tea gatherings for birthdays, bridal showers, or seasonal celebrations—complete with custom blends and beautiful presentation.

Corporate & Small Business Events: Elevate team meetings or client gatherings with curated tea service—iced teas, hot loose-leaf setups, or tastings.

Local Business & Market Collaborations: We love partnering with fellow makers for pop-ups, fairs, and events that strengthen local community ties.

Interested? Reach out today—we’d love to curate something special with you.

Herbal Wisdom & Tea Education

At CurAteas, every blend is a conversation between ancient herbal knowledge and modern wellness. We believe in making tea education accessible and inspiring—because knowing what’s in your cup is just as powerful as drinking it.

Our Ingredients, Our Intentions:

  • Lavender: Supports sleep and eases stress.
  • Rose: Promotes emotional harmony and antioxidant support.
  • Jasmine: Uplifting, aromatic, digestion-friendly.
  • White Tea: Gentle focus and antioxidant-rich.
  • Green Tea: Calm energy and clarity through L-theanine.
  • Black Tea: Bold, grounding, and energizing.

We’ll continue to share tea tips, herbal spotlights, and mindful sipping rituals. Every leaf tells a story—thank you for steeping into ours.
